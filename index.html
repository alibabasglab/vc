Instructions

1. Every question has a pair of utterances, the upper utterance in each pair is spoken by a target speaker.
2. Please listen to both utterances; try to disregard the spoken content and only focus on the voice/timbre of the utterances.
3. Compare the voice of the lower utterance to that of the upper one, and rate the similarity (how likely do you think the two voices belong to the same speaker.)

The ratings are on a 5-point scale:

Rating 1 means the two voices are totally different
Rating 5 means the two utterances are definitely produced by the same speaker
